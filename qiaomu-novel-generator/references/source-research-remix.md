# Source Research Remix Protocol

Use this file when the user explicitly asks to search first, names a recent or specific work whose public context matters, or asks for "similar to X, but change it into Y".

The purpose of search is not to copy the source. The purpose is to identify what readers recognize as the source's durable craft engine, then turn that engine into selectable, original story options.

## When To Search

Search before outlining when:

- The user says `联网搜`, `搜索`, `查资料`, `先了解`, `参考这个作品`, or equivalent.
- The user names a specific modern book, film, show, web novel, short drama, game, or public trope and wants the latest/recent public discussion.
- The assistant is unsure about the reference, publication details, plot premise, or audience reaction.
- The user wants more famous comparison beats to choose from.

If no search tool is available, say that current browsing is unavailable, then fall back to clearly labeled memory/general craft analysis.

## Search Scope

Use 3-6 public sources when practical:

1. Official or publisher/source page for stable premise facts.
2. Review or criticism page for structural interpretation.
3. Reader-response page for what felt "爽", funny, painful, or memorable.
4. Genre/craft source for general mechanics such as hooks, escalation, scene structure, or cliffhangers.
5. Optional industry/platform discussion when the target form is web fiction, short drama, serialized fiction, or workplace satire.

Do not rely on one snippet. Do not quote long passages. Do not use pirated full text, leaked scripts, or paywalled text as source material.

## Research Intake Card

Convert sources into this card before offering options:

```text
Reference signal:
Stable public premise:
Reader pleasure:
Core story function:
Conflict arena:
Pressure relationships:
Evidence objects / procedure objects:
Escalation pattern:
Style craft sliders:
Do-not-copy elements:
Fresh transformation levers:
```

Rules:

- `Stable public premise` should be short and factual.
- `Core story function` should be abstract: backstage fixer, public proof, long revenge, mistaken identity, institutional trap, etc.
- `Style craft sliders` must be generic. For living authors, never promise exact imitation.
- `Do-not-copy elements` must name what to avoid: characters, world names, unique scene order, signature lines, iconic objects, and protected passages.
- `Fresh transformation levers` should change at least setting, role system, relationship, central object/rule, stakes, and ending.

## Search-To-Remix Output

After searching, present compact options:

```text
我查到的可复用叙事功能是：

1. [功能 A]：[读者为什么会被抓住]
2. [功能 B]：[冲突如何升级]
3. [功能 C]：[爽点/反转如何释放]

可选桥段组合：
1A [经典信号] + [新题材变形]
2A [经典信号] + [新题材变形]
3A [经典信号] + [新题材变形]

我建议组合：[A + B + 一个反向处理]。
理由：[一句话]

确认后我给大纲，或你可以回复：按建议 / 更爽 / 更黑色幽默 / 更现实 / 更悬疑。
```

If the user has already asked for strategy and outline, include source links in a short `参考来源` line, then move to `小说如何吸引人` and `大纲`.

## Common Research-To-Story Adapters

| Public-source signal | Reusable function | Fresh transformation |
|---|---|---|
| 熟悉经典的后台视角 | 把台前英雄叙事改成后台执行者的压力链 | 换成现代组织、宗门、医院、太空站、平台治理、家族企业 |
| 大型联合项目 | 多方都说目标一致，私下各有考核和筹码 | 换成新品上线、灾害救援、继任审查、宗门盟约、殖民地迁移 |
| 办事流程喜剧 | 报告、审批、预算、名册、会议纪要成为笑点和证据 | 换成账册、算法日志、病历、祭文、案卷、拍卖目录 |
| 小人物夹在大系统里 | 主角没有最高权力，却最懂实际运转 | 让主角用经验、证据、时间差和人情债翻盘 |
| 表面任务与隐藏任务冲突 | 官方目标只是幌子，真正目标藏在责任、利益或旧案里 | 让主角发现项目成败都可能让自己背锅 |
| 组织内斗爽文 | 各派系抢功、甩锅、卡资源，主角把暗战拉到公开证据场 | 用公开审计、事故复盘、继任投票、黑箱测试释放爽点 |
| 荒诞制度讽刺 | 规则看似可笑但能真实伤人 | 让主角先被规则困住，再用规则迫使对手自证 |
| 职业修行 | 主角在处理麻烦中完成自我选择 | 结尾让一个早期无用物件或小原则改变意义 |

## Modern Organization Beat Cards

Offer these when the user wants workplace, business, big-organization, or office-politics fiction. Keep the labels as inspiration signals, not copy targets.

| Signal | Reusable function | Fresh transformation |
|---|---|---|
| Yes Minister 式 | 表面决策和真实权力相互错位，笑点来自话外之音 | 换成平台治理、宗门长老会、家族董事会、医院委员会 |
| Succession 式 | 继任权、血缘/资历、忠诚测试和公开羞辱缠在一起 | 缩小为一次投票、事故复盘、遗嘱宣读或资源分配会 |
| The Big Short 式 | 少数人看见系统性错误，别人把警告当笑话 | 换成模型评测、财务黑洞、灵脉枯竭、供应链断裂 |
| Spotlight 式 | 小团队用文件链条掀开被保护的真相 | 换成审计、病历、案卷、聊天记录、拍卖台账 |
| 12 Angry Men 式 | 一个小疑点在封闭评议中推翻多数人的确定性 | 换成评审会、宗门裁决、董事会、陪审团、家宴质证 |
| 食神式 | 失势者在底层重新理解手艺，最后公开翻身 | 换成组织边缘岗位、外包团队、基层门店、流放部门 |

## Case-Study Snapshot: Backstage Classic Into Workplace Satire

The public discussion around a famous "myth/classic as project management" novel points to these reusable lessons:

- Familiar canon can be refreshed by moving the camera away from the heroes and toward the person who must coordinate the impossible task.
- Project objects such as expenses, reports, personnel arrangements, favors, and hidden stakeholder motives can become both comedy and clue.
- The protagonist's pressure works because they lack absolute power but understand the system's actual joints.
- The best satire does not only mock procedures; it reveals how procedures decide who takes credit, who takes blame, and who keeps a conscience.
- Reader pleasure often comes from recognition: "this absurd immortal bureaucracy feels like my workplace", followed by a satisfying evidence-based reversal.

Useful public references checked on 2026-06-14:

- The Paper on the "major project" premise and workplace ecology: https://www.thepaper.cn/newsDetail_forward_23794714
- Hunan Today review on backstage POV, myth-to-workplace mapping, and many-sided institutional pressure: https://www.hunantoday.cn/news/xhn/202401/19382948.html
- Dedao reader-response page on office survival, implicit rules, stakeholder goals, and system inertia: https://www.dedao.cn/ebook/detail?id=A1De6QkaNRMlK5yxrdmnEBXY97ZL23n2Vgk3o6zvGDekj1QgA84VbpqJOPLgxEJl
- Short-drama craft reference on hook, reversal, sharp dialogue, escalation, and face-slapping sequence: https://www.cnblogs.com/VisionGo/p/19822785

## Boundary Check

Before drafting from searched sources:

- Have we changed setting, relationship, stakes, central object/rule, and ending?
- Are the cited works used as craft signals, not a copied chain of events?
- Are living-author style requests translated into generic craft sliders?
- Is the output offering choices before a full draft, unless the user explicitly asked to skip discussion?
- Is the story's main pleasure clear:爽、笑、惊、燃、痛、甜, or黑色余味?
